About Us

Together, we can transform the educational landscape of Rural India

90% of Indian education system is driven by rural Government schools.We face two chronic challenges in our Indian education system – acute teacher shortage and teaching quality. A grade 5 student’s literacy rate is extremely low, and they are unable to read a grade 2 level textbook.


Therefore, with limited access to quality education, we see children between grades 5-8 discontinuing their education, and prioritising earning a livelihood to support their families.

Our goal is to continue to support these children, and reach out to more, so that no child has to drop out of school.
